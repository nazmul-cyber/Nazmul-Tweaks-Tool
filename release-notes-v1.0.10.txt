Tweak Back panel — pick which tweaks to revert.

**Home UI**
- Removed "Restore Previous Settings" batch buttons
- Left side: **Tweak Back** with checkboxes for every applied tweak
- Check = revert, uncheck = keep
- **All** / **None** / **Revert Checked** buttons

**Behavior**
- Reverted tweaks are removed from saved history
- Panel refreshes after apply, revert, or returning to Home
- Tweaks without undo script show as "(manual only)" and stay disabled
